Why Caddie

Designed for how engineers actually work.

Caddie is built around three principles that most AI tools miss entirely.

📊
Context-Aware AI

Before answering, Caddie reads your feature tree, active configuration, selection state, rebuild errors, and takes a live viewport screenshot. Every answer references your actual model — not a textbook example.

🔄
Always Up to Date

Caddie notifies you inside SolidWorks when a new version is ready. One download, ten seconds to apply — no re-registration needed. You never need to check a website.

🔒
Private by Default

All conversation history, notes, and preferences are stored in SQLite on your PC. No account needed, no telemetry, no analytics — confidential designs stay confidential.

💬
Smart Chat

Ask anything: "Why does my loft fail?", "Add K-factor to sheet metal", "Review my feature tree." Caddie answers specifically about your model.

🎓
CSWP → CSWE Training

Structured exercises, step-by-step guidance, and review rubrics aligned with Dassault's certification syllabi.

🖼
Teach Me From Image

Drop a screenshot of any model and Caddie generates a complete SolidWorks build plan with ordered feature steps.

🔍
Model Review

One-click review surfaces rebuild errors, naming issues, dangling references, and design intent improvements — prioritized by severity.

How It Works

SolidWorks-native. No tab switching.

Caddie runs entirely inside SolidWorks as a native taskpane add-in. Your data stays local. There's no browser, no copy-paste.

1

Install the add-in

Extract the zip, right-click install.bat → Run as Administrator, enable Caddie under Tools → Add-Ins. No account needed.

2

Open your model

Open any part, assembly, or drawing. Caddie automatically captures a context snapshot — feature tree, configuration, selection, and viewport.

3

Ask anything

Type your question. Caddie responds with specific advice referencing your actual feature names, sketches, and mates.

Privacy First

Your CAD data stays on your machine.

Caddie is built for engineers who work on confidential designs. We take data security seriously by default.

💾
Local Storage

All history, notes, and preferences are stored in SQLite on your PC. Nothing leaves without your permission.

🔑
No Account Needed

Caddie includes its own AI access during alpha. No OpenAI account or API key required — just install and go.

📵
No Telemetry

Zero usage tracking by default. No analytics, no crash reporting unless you explicitly opt in.

🏠
Opt-in Cloud

Cloud sync for history (coming in M6) is always explicit opt-in, never automatic.

Download Caddie Alpha

Ready to run inside SolidWorks 2022+. Extract the zip, run the installer, and you're chatting with your models in under 5 minutes.

⇓  Download CaddieAlpha.zip

Windows 10 / 11  ·  64-bit  ·  SolidWorks 2022 or newer  ·  free during alpha

1
Extract the zip to a permanent folder — e.g. C:\Caddie\
Don’t leave it in Downloads; the DLL path must stay fixed.
2
Right-click install.bat → Run as Administrator
Click Yes on the UAC prompt. You’ll see “SUCCESS: Caddie registered.”
3
In SolidWorks: Tools → Add-Ins → Caddie → check Active & Start Up → OK
The “Ask Caddie” panel appears on the right. Open any model and start chatting.
